Founder

SRIMATHI RANI ADAIKALARAJ EDUCATIONAL & CHARITABLE TRUST is a registered trust which was established in the year 1994 to serve the youth of our Motherland through education. Srimathi Rani Adaikalaraj Educational & charitable Trust was created by our honourable founder Shri L.Adaikalaraj (Late), the former M.P. from Tiruchirappalli constituency. The present Managing Trustee & Correspondent of Jenneys Group of Institutions and JENNEY’S COLLEGE OF EDUCATION is Thiru. A.Joseph Francis, a man of simplicity but a dynamic personality with the twin views of Education for the masses and serving the society through education. To champion the cause of justice & human dignity and to overcome emerging challenges of society, our Trust started Jenney’s College of Physical Education offering B.P.Ed programme approved by NCTE and affiliated to Tamil Nadu Physical Education & Sports University, Chennai. The society-conscious trustees of Srimathi Rani Adaikalaraj Educational & charitable Trust have established Jenney’s Academy College of Hotel Management, affiliated to Alagappa University, Karaikudi; Jenney’s College of Nursing (B.Sc) affiliated to Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai and Jenney’s college of Education which is approved by the National Council for Teachers Education and affiliated to Tamil Nadu Teachers Education university, Chennai.

I wish and pray that our students should rise up to be the best versions of themselves and realise all their dreams.

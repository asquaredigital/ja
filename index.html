Why choose us?

  • The quality of faculty members is one of the most significant assets of our institution. Our experienced and knowledgeable faculty members provide students with a good-quality education and training that prepares them for careers in the hospitality industry.

  • A well-designed and up-to-date curriculum is essential for an institution to provide students with the knowledge and skills required to succeed in the hospitality industry. The Alagappa University, Karaikudi has designed an excellent curriculum for the Under Graduate Program, Diploma and Craft Certificate Programs. The curriculum covers all the aspects of Hotel Management, including Food and Beverage Service, Housekeeping, Front Office management, Marketing, Budgeting and more.

  • JAHM has modern and well-equipped classrooms with latest teaching aids, large training kitchens, training restaurants, and guest rooms that provide students with a practical learning experience. State-of-the-art facilities are provided to enhance the students efficiency and institution's reputation.

  • In collaboration with the hospitality industry we provide students with real-world experience, exposure to industry trends, and access to employment opportunities. These partnerships can also provide the institution with resources such as guest lectures, industry insights, and innovation in food industry.

  • We also have a strong and engaged alumni network. This network is spread worldwide because our alumni are working in many parts of the world, and the major reason being, our institution has been producing hoteliers since 1996. Our Alumni serve as our ambassadors and help the budding hoteliers.

  • These assets of JAHM will help the students to acquire a good-quality education and training added with the goodwill and reputation of the institution.

A: After completing the course, the candidate can expect a range of career prospects in the hospitality industry. Some potential career paths include Hotel operational trainee or as individual department employee, Management trainee and as On the job trainee. If the student's communication and quantum of technical knowledge and skill is commendable, then the hotels can absorb the student as a team leader in any one of the 4 major departments whichever the student specializes. With a hotel management background, the student could specialize in event planning and coordination, organizing conferences, weddings, meetings, and other special events within hotels or event management companies. The student would focus on promoting the hotel's services, attracting new customers, managing client relationships, developing marketing strategies, and driving revenue through sales efforts. The student could become a revenue manager and would analyze market trends, set pricing strategies, manage inventory and distribution channels, and optimize revenue for the hotel by maximizing room rates and occupancy. With experience and expertise in hotel management, the student could work as a consultant, providing advisory services to hotels, resorts, or hospitality organizations, offering guidance on operations, efficiency, profitability, and guest satisfaction. Armed with knowledge of hotel management, the student may consider starting the student's own hotel or hospitality-related businesses, such as a boutique hotel, bed and breakfast, or a specialized service within the industry. These are just a few examples of the career prospects available after completing a hotel management course. The hospitality industry offers diverse opportunities for growth, and with experience and continuous learning, the student can progress into higher-level management positions or explore other areas of the industry
